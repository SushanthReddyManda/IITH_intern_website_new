Virtual Experience Design Lab

Virtual Experience Design

The lab intends to explore virtual dimensions of perception and visualization for research in
architecture and design. The lab will connect academics and research for
fresh and innovative ideas in to this domain.

The output could be in the form of virtual reality, augmented reality,
mixed reality for an immersive experience to the user.

The lab would look for fitting applications for virtual architecture experience & projection computations for optimization of system, form, material and processes, etc.
